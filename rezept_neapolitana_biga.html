neapolitana with biga.

fermented 2x24h in the fridge.

  • 1000g italian pizza flour tipo 00 (i use caputo cuoco)
  • 500ml lukewarm water
  • 2g fresh yeast

disolve yeast in water, shake all ingredients in bowl until water is soaked in
let rise for 30 minutes and place for 24h in the fridge

  • 200ml icecold water
  • 30g salt
